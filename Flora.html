Subnautica has a large collection of different flora organisms.

Acid Mushroom

These mushrooms grow almost everywhere. They are collectable by hand or by knife, just be aware that if you cut them, they will release poisonous spores capable of damaging the player. They are used to create batteries.

Bloodroot

These roots usually grow in Blood Kelp caves. They frequently have Blood Oil capsules on them, that the player can collect. They are used to create benzene.

Bloodvine

These species grow in the Blood Kelp zone. They grow Blood Oil.

Blue Palm

Blue Palm is purely decorative flora species. They can be found in shallow biomes. You can harvest Blue Palm seeds and plant them.

Bulb Bush

Bulb Bushes are only found in the Bulb zone. You can harvest their edible samples with knife. Used in Hatching Enzymes.

Bulbo Tree

Bulbo Trees are terrestrial flora found on the floating islands. Their samples are edible and viable sources of food and water.

Cave Bush

They are found in the Jellyshroom Caves, Mountains Caves, and on the Sea Treader's Path. You can harvest their seeds using knife.

Chinese Potato Plant

They are non-native terrestrial species found on the floating islands. It is the source of the Chinese Potato.

Creepvine

Grows exclusively in the Kelp Forest. They grow seeds heavily used in crafting.

Deep Shroom

Subspecies of Acid Mushrooms found much deeper. They also damage player on impact so be careful.

Eye Stalk

Flora species found in deep biomes as well as the Kelp Forest Caves. You can harvest Eye Stalk Seeds by using knife. Used to create Hatching Enzymes.

Fern Palm

Decorative bio-engineered terrestrial flora species.

Furled Papyrus

Purely decorative species.

Gabe's Feather

Decorative species found in the Blood Kelp zone.

Gel Sack

Gel sacks are part of the edible flora. Grows on the walls of caves. Used to create aerogel.

Ghost Weed

Commonly found in the Lost River. It's used to create Hatching Enzymes.

Grub Basket

Terrestrial flora species found exclusively on the Floating Island. It is purely decorative.

Jaffa Cup

Another decorative flora species found on the Floating Island.

Jellyshroom

Flora species found in the Jellyshroom Cave. They are home to the Crabsnakes. Harvestable using knife.

Lantern Tree

Rare terrestrial flora species found in a small section of the Floating Island. It grows Lantern Fruit - one of the best sources of food in the game.

Marblemelon Plant

Another terrestrial life form. You can harvest its seeds using knife. You can also eat its melons.

Membrain Tree

Grows on rocks of the Grand Reef. It is purely decorative.

Ming Plant

Exclusively found on the Floating Island. You can harvest its seeds.

Pink Cap

Terrestrial flora found on the floating islands. The player can use its seeds in the bioreactor.

Purple Pinecone

Very rare species. Can be harvested for salt deposits.

Pygmy Fan

Small plant found in the Mushroom Forest and in the Bulb Zone. Can be harvested for its seeds.

Redwort

Found in many of the shallow biomes. Can be harvested for seeds.

Regress Shell

Flora species found in deeper biomes. It is purely decorative.

Rouge Cradle

Large flora species that serves as a light source. Can be harvested using knife.

Sea Crown

One of the rarest types of harvestable flora in Subnautica. Found in the Bulb zone, Dunes sinkhole, Jellyshroom caves and the Sea Emperors aquarium. Harvestable using knife.

Speckled Rattler

Small, terrestrial flora species found on the floating islands.
